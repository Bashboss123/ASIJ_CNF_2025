木漏れ日

Komorebi

What is

?

Komorebi is a Japanese word that captures the interplay of sunlight filtering through the leaves of trees, creating shifting patterns of light and shadow. It evokes a sense of fleeting beauty, tranquility, and the quiet poetry of nature—an ephemeral moment where light and life intertwine.

It is composed of three characters in Japanese:

木

ko

"Tree," symbolizing growth, roots, and stability.

漏れ

mo-re

"Leak" or "Filter," representing movement, fluidity, and subtle transitions.

日

bi

"Sun" or "Light," signifying warmth, enlightenment, and the passage of time.
