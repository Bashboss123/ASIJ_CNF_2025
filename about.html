About SUPA Creative Non-Fiction

SUPA Creative Non-Fiction is dedicated to exploring the power of storytelling through real-life experiences. We combine literary artistry with factual accuracy to create compelling narratives that resonate with readers.

What is Creative Non-Fiction?

Creative Non-Fiction (CNF) is a genre that merges literary techniques with factual storytelling. It allows writers to present real events in an engaging and artistic way, making facts more compelling.
